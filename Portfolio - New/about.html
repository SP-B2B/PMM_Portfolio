About

Product communications professional, transitioning into Product Marketing.

I'm a Product Communications and Technical Marketing professional with five years of experience helping cybersecurity and B2B SaaS companies turn complex products into clear market positions.

My work sits at the intersection of product, marketing, and sales — translating technical capability into the language buyers actually use to make decisions. I've built positioning and messaging frameworks from zero, led website narrative architecture, developed buyer personas for security and enterprise audiences, and created the sales enablement and thought leadership content that supports those narratives in-market.

Most of that experience lives in cybersecurity. I currently lead product communications and messaging strategy at CyberMindr, an exposure management platform operating in the CTEM and Attack Surface Management space. Before that, I worked on i-Radar, the product that became CyberMindr, building early positioning and customer-facing documentation across vulnerability management, penetration testing, and risk assessment. Earlier, at Blufig, I led messaging and content strategy for more than 18 B2B SaaS companies across cloud, enterprise software, and emerging technology — work that included repositioning a robotics company toward a software-first SaaS narrative and supporting the launch of a workplace engagement platform from the ground up.

That range — deep in one category, broad across many — is what's pulling me toward Product Marketing. I've spent five years doing the strategic work PMMs do: positioning, messaging, competitive analysis, GTM communications, sales enablement, thought leadership. What I'm looking for now is the title, scope, and cross-functional seat at the table to match.

I hold a B.Com from the University of Calcutta, along with certifications in IBM Enterprise Design Thinking and Google Technical Writing. I'm based in Hyderabad, India, and open to remote roles globally.
